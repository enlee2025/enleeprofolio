Titansoft · SBOTOP
VIP 分層系統——從零到一建立留存機制 VIP Tier System — Building Retention from Scratch
高價值用戶流失至競品，平台缺乏差異化留存手段。設計跨產品線、多國家的 VIP 分層機制，以數據說服營運團隊接受自動化。 High-value users were churning to competitors with no differentiated retention in place. Designed a multi-country, cross-product VIP tier system and used data to win over skeptical ops teams.
留存設計Retention Design 跨團隊影響力Stakeholder Management 多市場Multi-market
+18%
VIP 用戶留存率（3個月同群對比）VIP retention (3-month cohort)
問題Problem
高價值用戶因缺乏差異化對待而流失至競品，老客戶抱怨並離開，平台沒有任何正式的留存與獎勵機制。 High-value users churned to competitors due to lack of differentiated treatment. Long-time users complained and left. The platform had no formal retention or reward mechanism in place.
做法Approach
  • 分析競品 VIP 機制，交叉比對自家用戶在 Sports / Casino / Slots 三條產品線的下注行為
  • 以每週下注量為核心分層指標，針對不同國家、不同產品線設計獨立級距邏輯
  • 上線前每週提供「預估 VIP 用戶表現報表」，預測給予專屬優惠帶來的下注與儲值提升，逐步建立營運團隊對自動化的信任
  • Benchmarked competitor VIP programs and cross-analyzed user betting behavior across Sports, Casino, and Slots product lines
  • Used weekly betting volume as the core tiering metric, designing country-specific and product-specific tier logic
  • Built weekly "projected VIP performance" reports pre-launch to demonstrate expected uplift and earn ops team buy-in on automation
挑戰Challenge
多國家 × 多產品線的商業邏輯極度複雜；營運團隊初期抗拒自動化，需先用數據說服再推進系統。上線後短期內用戶大量詢問機制細節，後以 FAQ 與站內說明解決。 Business logic was highly complex across multiple countries and product lines. The ops team initially resisted automation — data-driven persuasion was needed before any system could move forward. Post-launch, a spike in user inquiries added short-term workload, resolved by deploying in-product FAQs.
成果Outcome
同群 VIP 用戶前後對比（3 個月週期），留存率提升 18%。 Retention rate increased by 18% among the same VIP user cohort over a 3-month comparison window.

AI Churn Prediction——將流失預測整合進 CRM AI Churn Prediction — Integrating Behavioral Models into CRM
CRM 靠人工判斷流失風險，效率低、覆蓋不完整。主導自建 AI 模型需求定義，設計雙軌自動介入機制，以 A/B test 驗證效果。 CRM team relied on manual judgment to identify churn risk — inefficient and incomplete. Led requirement definition for an in-house AI model and designed a dual-track auto-intervention system, validated by A/B test.

問題Problem
CRM 團隊靠人工判斷流失風險，效率低、覆蓋不完整，高價值用戶在缺乏即時介入的情況下悄悄離開。 The CRM team relied on manual risk assessment, leaving gaps in coverage. High-value users quietly churned without timely intervention.
做法Approach
  • 作為 PM 定義模型需求，整合營運業務邏輯與 Data team 技術能力，協助建立自有 AI churn prediction 模型
  • 設計雙軌觸發機制：自動發送優惠券（高風險用戶即時挽留）＋生成清單通知營運人員手動跟進
  • 上線前三個月在特定市場對 50% 用戶進行 A/B test
  • Defined model requirements as PM, bridging ops business logic and Data team's technical capabilities to build an in-house AI churn prediction model
  • Designed a dual-track trigger system: auto-send vouchers for high-risk users + generate watch-lists for ops to manually follow up on high-value users
  • Ran a 3-month A/B test on 50% of users in a target market pre-launch
成果Outcome
A/B test 驗證：AI 介入組用戶留存提升 20%（對照組同期數據）。 A/B test confirmed: the AI-intervention group showed 20% higher retention vs. the control group over the same period.

問題Problem
新用戶幾乎全靠行銷渠道獲取，CAC 高且缺乏有機成長動能，註冊後到首次下注的轉換率偏低。 New users were almost entirely sourced from paid marketing channels with high CAC and no organic growth engine. Post-registration to first-bet conversion was low.

  • Designed a two-tier commission structure: A refers B, A earns more as B deposits more; B refers C, A also earns a second-tier bonus
  • Integrated the AI team's Duplicate User Checking API (personal info + behavior patterns + IP) to prevent bonus abuse via duplicate accounts
成果Outcome
註冊後首次下注轉換率提升 5%。 Post-registration to first-bet conversion rate increased by 5%.

Euro Cup 活動——整合預熱設計提升賽後留存 Euro Cup Campaign — Pre-event Warm-up to Improve Post-event Retention
大型賽事流量集中爆發，活動結束後新戶快速流失。設計賽前 2 個月預測遊戲預熱機制，整合四條產品線統一 Landing Page。 Major event traffic spiked during the tournament but new users churned quickly afterward. Designed a 2-month pre-event prediction game warm-up with a unified landing page across four product lines.

+10%
新戶活動後留存率（vs 上屆）Post-event new user retention (vs. prior edition)
問題Problem
大型賽事期間各產品線各自為政，用戶體驗分散；缺乏賽前預熱機制，流量集中在賽事期間爆發，活動結束後新戶流失嚴重。 Product lines operated independently during major events, creating a fragmented user experience. Without pre-event engagement, traffic peaked only during the tournament and new users churned heavily afterward.
做法Approach
  • 建立統一 Landing Page，整合 Sports / Casino / Slots / Games 四條產品線，作為單一廣告入口
  • 賽事開始前 2 個月上線 Prediction Game：玩家從小組賽預測到決賽，預測成功可兌現獎勵——但前提是提早在 Sports 頁面下注，驅動賽前投注行為
  • 協調技術團隊確保系統架構能承載高併發流量
  • Built a unified landing page integrating Sports, Casino, Slots, and Games as a single ad destination
  • Launched a Prediction Game 2 months before the tournament: players predicted match results from group stage to final; correct predictions could be redeemed — but only if they placed early bets on the Sports page, driving pre-event betting behavior
  • Coordinated with engineering to ensure system architecture could handle high-concurrency traffic during the event
成果Outcome
活動期間用戶數相比上屆 Euro Cup 成長 5%；活動結束後新戶留存率提升 10%，說明預熱機制有效提升黏著度。 Active users during the event grew 5% vs. the previous Euro Cup edition. Post-event new user retention improved by 10%, demonstrating that the warm-up mechanism effectively built lasting engagement.
